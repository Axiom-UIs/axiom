## ğŸ’¡ The Problem
Patients with chronic diseases often struggle to juggle multiple aspects of their health:
* Tracking complex medication schedules.
* Understanding specific nutritional needs (e.g., low sugar/sodium).
* Monitoring vitals and sharing them effectively with healthcare providers.
* Lack of motivation or emotional support.

## ğŸš€ The Solution
Based on our research, we designed **Medico** to serve three distinct user roles: **Patients, Doctors, and Caregivers**.

## ğŸ› ï¸ Design Process
Our team followed the Design Thinking methodology to ensure a user-centric solution:
1.  **Empathize:** User research and competitor analysis.
2.  **Define:** Creating User Personas (Patients & Caregivers) and Empathy Maps.
3.  **Ideate:** Mind mapping (Architecture), User Flows, and Wireframing.
4.  **Prototype:** High-fidelity UI design in Figma.
5.  **Test:** Usability testing and iteration.
